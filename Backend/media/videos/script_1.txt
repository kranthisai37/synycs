# Introduction to Machine Learning  Hello everyone.  In this
video, we will learn about Machine Learning, one of the most
important fields in modern technology and artificial
intelligence.  Machine Learning, often called ML, is a
branch of AI that enables computers to learn from data and
improve their performance without being explicitly
programmed for every task.  Instead of writing fixed rules,
we train machines using examples and patterns from data.
